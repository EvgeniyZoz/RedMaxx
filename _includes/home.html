Introduction

Who can benefit from this bootcamp?

  • Career changers

    For those seeking a career change, explore the realm of UX/UI design – a profession that harmonies creative expression with analytical thinking.

  • Early career professionals

    For recent graduates and early-career professionals aspiring to improve skills and progress to mid-weight UX/UI roles, we provide the essential expertise to excel in the field.

  • Tech novices

    Individuals who have a limited background in technology or design and are interested in acquiring digital design skills to excel as mid-weight designers.

  • Web and graphic designers

    Organise and consolidate your knowledge, enrich your portfolio with diverse projects, and cultivate a heightened sense of confidence within the competitive market landscape.

Skills you will gain

Job-ready skills employers seek

  • Research

    You'll discover user preferences and needs in product usage. You'll master competitor analysis, gather insights, analyse your target audience, and craft personas or user archetypes.

  • Prototyping

    You'll master creating interactive prototypes for various devices. These prototypes, with different levels of detail, will cover various scenarios, helping you confirm design choices.

  • Visual design

    You will learn how to design clear, consistent, and appealing visual design, to enhance usability, encourage user actions, and set your product apart.

  • Testing

    You will learn how to test hypotheses and evaluate different design decisions using various testing methodologies, including usability, accessibility, guerrilla testing, card sorting, and more.

Possible career paths

  • UX/UI Designer

    Create user-friendly interfaces and experiences for websites, mobile apps, and digital platforms, prioritising ease of use and attractive design.

  • Visual Designer

    Focus on creating visually appealing designs by selecting colour schemes, typography, and other visual elements that enhance the overall aesthetic.

  • UX Researcher

    Conduct user research to gather insights, which aids in shaping the design process and ensures that products align with user requirements

  • Product Designer

    Engage in end-to-end product design, from conceptualisation to final execution, considering both the user experience and aesthetics.

Curriculum

Design Excellence Unleashed: What You'll Learn in our Ui/UX Bootcamp

In this bootcamp, you'll adopt design thinking, learn AI techniques, and enhance your ability to create personalised and compelling user experiences. Our focus is on practical tasks that cover design principles, teaching you both UI and UX fundamentals and advanced techniques. AI learning is seamlessly woven throughout the program, empowering you to boost your performance and results using modern AI language. Our aim is to shape well-rounded mid-weight designers with holistic skills.
